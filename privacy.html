Privacy Notice

Draft for pre-launch review. This notice must be reviewed before collecting real leads.

What we collect

We may collect business contact details, province, sector, services offered, tender preferences, and optional company-profile material provided by the business.

Why we collect it

We use this information to match tender opportunities, prepare sample reports, send deadline reminders, and manage subscription enquiries.

How we store it

Pre-launch data is stored in controlled workspace files. Production data will need access control, backup rules, retention rules, and deletion handling.

Sharing

We do not sell customer information. We only share information if required to provide the service, comply with law, or with explicit customer instruction.

Customer control

Customers can request correction or deletion of their information. A production contact address must be added before launch.
