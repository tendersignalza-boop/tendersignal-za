The problem

Tenders are scattered, technical, and easy to miss.

Most owner-managed businesses do not have a bid office. Opportunities sit across portals, municipal pages, universities, SOEs, and supplier portals. By the time a business finds the right tender, the closing date is close and the document list is unclear.

TenderSignal ZA turns that daily monitoring burden into a subscription report workflow.

How it works

From messy portals to a clear weekly pipeline.

01

Profile your business

Sector, province, services, compliance status, deal size, and buyer preferences.

02

Monitor opportunities

Public procurement sources are checked for matching opportunities and closing dates.

03

Summarise the fit

Each match gets a plain-English brief, required-document list, risks, and bid/no-bid signal.

04

Prepare faster

Receive response outlines, clarification questions, and weekly pipeline reports.
